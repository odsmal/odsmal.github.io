My name is Kristoffer and I make digital user interfaces for physical products. My design solutions reside in products ranging from fighter jets to production line equipment.

Forward leaning

I take charge of the thing I'm designing, I care about it and I want it to result in a product that is well thought out and fit for its purpose.

User centric

I keep the users invovled no matter where I am in the process of building their tool. I design for them and for what they want to accomplish.

Persistent

A design processes is an adventure with ups and downs. It won't always be a walk in the park. But one should always keep walking.
