WEATHER APP

PUBLISHED: MARS 2022
Left: chart with weather parameters Right: precipitation data visualized over a map

So the actual background for creating this weather app, besides my nerdy urge to create something involving precipitation radar data, is that my household wanted a place to easily spot time and temperature while at home. That is of course why the two white windows with current temprature and time takes up a good amount of space in the UI. The graph serves as a serious decision making tool when dressing my young one for the kindergarden and the map with precipitation radar data is just a delight to look at. Visit the GitHub repo to see what the weather app is made of.
