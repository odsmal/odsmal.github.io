WEATHER APP

PUBLISHED: MARS 2022
Left: chart with weather parameters Right: precipitation data visualized over a map

I went into this project with a nerdy urge to create something involving precipitation radar data. I mean who doesn’t like to visit regnradar.se when they see a rain cloud to better understand how much rain they’re looking at? Nerdy urges aside, the offical reason for creating the weather app was that my household wanted a place to easily spot time and temperature in the home. That is of course why the two white windows with current temprature and time takes up a good amount of space in the UI; they´re easily visible from different angles and distances even when shown on a small display. The graph serves as a serious decision making tool when dressing my young one for the kindergarden and the map with precipitation radar data is just a delight to look at. Visit the GitHub repo to see what the weather app is made of.
